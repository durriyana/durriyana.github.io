💥 PART-TIME WORKING EXPERIENCES 💥:

How did i start? And how can i manage my time as a student while doing part time work?

At first, im doing my part-time job just to fill up my free time. Among the many new skills I picked up how to make pizza, prepare ingredients, and serve clients. Recalling all of the difficulties and problems that must be patiently overcome. I would like to try working again in the future though because I truly enjoyed it.
